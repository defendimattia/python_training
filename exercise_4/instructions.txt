Welcome. In this kata, you are asked to square every digit of a number and concatenate them.

For example, if we run 9119 through the function, 811181 will come out, because square of 9 is 81 and square of 1 is 1. (81-1-1-81)

Example #2: An input of 765 will/should return 493625 because square of 7 is 49, square of 6 is 36, and square of 5 is 25. (49-36-25)

Note: The function accepts an integer and returns an integer.

Happy Coding!
